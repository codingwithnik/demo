Prakash Alloy Udyog

“Prakash Alloy Udyog” are one of the leading manufacturers and exporter of Aluminium Tope, Aluminium Parat, Aluminium Cookware and various other kitchen utensils. These products are manufactured by use of high-grade raw material procured from trusted sources. Moreover, we conduct several quality tests to ensure the production of flawless products. Our state of the art manufacturing facility is located in the Industrial Estate of Manakpur, Jagadhri Distt Yamunanagar, Haryana (India). The aluminium utensil industry in Jagadhri can be traced back to early 20th Century and continues to thrive even today.
